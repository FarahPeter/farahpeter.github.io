01 About

Engineering at the edge of the network

I'm a network and infrastructure engineer specialising in low-level networking, infrastructure automation, and threat intelligence. I hold a Master's in Cybersecurity from the Institut Polytechnique de Paris (graduated with highest distinction) and a Bachelor's in Computer and Communication Engineering from the American University of Beirut.

Currently, I am an Infrastructure Automation Engineer at Murex, working with Ansible, Jenkins, Git, and Kubernetes. My broader experience spans managing enterprise network infrastructure at Dar, integrating automated security monitoring pipelines at Exeo, and developing advanced Active Queue Management (AQM) classification frameworks during my research internships. Across these roles I've turned complex infrastructure and security problems into automated, observable systems — from CI/CD pipelines to hardened enterprise networks.
